About us

The Green Nigeria Movement GNM is an initiative of the Nigerian Young Professionals Forum NYPF that seeks to enlighten voters on the need to participate actively in elections and mobilize over 12 Million votes at all levels to vote in the 2023 Nigerian presidential Elections.
